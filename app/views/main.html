Add-1 is a mental exercise that forces your focus to its limits.

Click to Begin

Rules

Your objective is to add 1 to each digit of the given number. Ex 9304 -> 0415
You will have 5 seconds to do so in the beginning. Answering while the timer bar is still green counts for 2.

Concept

This is a famous exercise mentioned by Kahneman in his book Thinking Fast and Slow,
Kahneman states that the most effortful thing you can do is the Add1 exercise.

Goal

Practice pushing your focus to its limit and experiencing the discomfort of effort.
Train your mind to focus with less effort.
